Usage

Display the tooltip as long as the user long-presses or hovers over the element. The tooltip appears with a delay of 400 milliseconds. Don’t use a tooltip containing more than 70 characters. If you need to use more than 70 characters, you should use a hint.

A tooltip appears in the center of the click target, below it. If there isn’t enough space the tooltip can appear over click targets, in the rignt or left of click targets.

If tooltips under or above the target of the click, use center alignment. If it on the left or on the right of the target of the click, use left or right alignment.

Hints

A hint is a additional info that a user opens by hover on or clicking or on an element. The hint appears with a delay of 100 milliseconds. The hints always applies a left alignment.

Tooltip

Tooltip display brief text that gives additional information and appear when user hover over, focus on, or tap an element.

Display the tooltip as long as the user long-presses or hovers over the element.
The tooltip appears with a delay of 400 milliseconds.
Don’t use a tooltip containing more than 70 characters. If you need to use more than 70 characters, you should use a hint.

A tooltip appears in the center of the click target, below it.

If the tooltip is near to the icon, the space between them should increase to 16 px.

The maximum width of a tooltip’s area is 240 px.

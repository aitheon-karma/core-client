Snackbars

Snackbars provide brief messages about an operation.

Snackbars without action dissapear after 3 sec. Snackbars with action dissapear after 7 sec, and always contains a cross for manual exit. Only one snackbar may be displayed at the time. The recommended size is 344px. But if necessary, the snackbar can may be wider

Snackbar places in the left bottom corner of the page with bottom-padding and left-padding in 24 px.

If snackbar contains important information about connecting to the internet or server errors, it is places at the top of page with top-padding in 120 px and center alignment.

For desktop and tablet should use only one line of text in snackbars.

Snackbars can contain loaders or icon provides system states.

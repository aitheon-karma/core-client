Elevations

Shadows helps create elevation. Elevation is the relative distance between two surfaces.

Use the shadow if you want to show that one element is higher than the other one. In the Aitheon design system exist two types of shadows.

Small Shadow

Usage: Tooltips, hints, banners, sneakbars

-webkit-box-shadow: 0px 2px 6px 0px rgba(31,31,31,1);

-moz-box-shadow: 0px 2px 6px 0px rgba(31,31,31,1);

box-shadow: 0px 2px 6px 0px rgba(31,31,31,1);

$small-elevation-dt

X 0

Y 2

Blur 6

Color $coal-gray (1f1f1f)

Opacity 100%

Big Shadow

Usage: Dialogs

-webkit-box-shadow: 0px 4px 12px 0px rgba(31,31,31,1);

-moz-box-shadow: 0px 4px 12px 0px rgba(31,31,31,1);

box-shadow: 0px 4px 12px 0px rgba(31,31,31,1);

$small-elevation-dt

X 0

Y 4

Blur 12

Color $coal-gray (1f1f1f)

Opacity 100%
